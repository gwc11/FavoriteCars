The Soap Bar

To be honest, I dont really have a specific story behind why I like this car like the other ones. This is a car that I gradually got exposed to more and more throughout different racing games and YouTube videos, and it just kinda caught my eye. Every racing game I play nowadays I always buy it, customize it, and race it to my hearts content. Sometimes I'll leave it stock and just change the color of it, othertimes I'll re-create Keisuke Takahashi's RX-7 from Initial D. Whether it's the MX-5 from the 90s with the pop up headlights, a fully customized RX-7 race car, or a more recent, slicker looking MX-5, I just really like the car. Part of me kind of want to own one, another part of me wants more space in a car I'm going to drive daily...
